MEALSTORM

Mealstorm, a frantic roguelite where you step into the shoes of a brave cook on a mission to save the culinary kingdom from crazed, rebellious vegetables! Dive into a series of quick runs and climb the leaderboard to prove you're the best! What are you waiting for? Jump into the fray!

Every run is unique: explore new maps, face increasingly tough enemies, unlock new utensils and master the art of culinary combat.

With fast-paced battles, an engaging progression system and unique features, Mealstorm offers hours of fun for fans of roguelites and arcade action!
